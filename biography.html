My Biography

I was born on December 12, 2003, in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. I currently study Digital Journalism at the Univeristy of Ottawa. My favourite hobbies are skating and baking. I do not play ringette competitively but I volunteer with a group that gives kids with disabilities the chance to play ringette, called Ringette For All. My favourite things to bake are:

  • Cupcakes
  • No-bake cheesecake
  • Christmas cookies
